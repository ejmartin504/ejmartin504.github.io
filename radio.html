  • June 9, 2019 - Dr. John Tribute

    Sadly, the audio for this show did not get properly recorded, but perhaps that is fitting...
    This one was particularly meaningful for me. Dr. John's passing hit me hard, and I wanted to honor him the only way I know: by playing and enjoying his music and the music of his contemporaries. It was a lot of fun for me to find out more about this remarkable man, and about the world that made him. I particularly enjoyed finding this clip of him describing his music, which I played on air at the beginning and end of the set.
